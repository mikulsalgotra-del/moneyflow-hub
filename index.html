Action Plan

Step-by-Step
Roadmap

Follow these steps in order. Most people who fail skip steps 1–3 and wonder why their ads earn nothing.

01

Pick a High-RPM Niche

Finance, health, legal, or tech. Don't chase passion — chase intent. Advertisers pay for visitors who are about to spend money. A finance blog with 5k visitors beats a food blog with 50k visitors in ad revenue.

FinanceHealthLegalSaaS
02

Build SEO-First Content

Target long-tail keywords with buying intent: "best credit card for students 2026", "personal injury lawyer cost", "cheapest health insurance plan." Each article is an asset that earns while you sleep. Aim for 50+ articles before monetizing.

Long-tail SEOBuying intent50+ articles
03

Drive US/UK/CA/AU Traffic

Ad networks pay 5–20× more for English-speaking Western traffic. Indian or Southeast Asian traffic brings CPMs under $0.50. Use Pinterest, Google SEO, and Reddit to attract the right visitors before applying to ad networks.

USA trafficUK / CA / AUGoogle SEO
04

Apply to Premium Ad Networks

Start with Google AdSense (any size). Once you hit 50k sessions/month, apply to Mediavine (pays 3–5× more). At 100k pageviews try Raptive. These networks optimize placements automatically to maximize your RPM.

AdSense → Mediavine → Raptive
05

Optimize Ad Placements

Place your best ad (300×250 or 336×280) directly after your first paragraph — it sees 100% of readers. Add a sticky sidebar and a mobile sticky footer. These three zones alone account for 60–70% of total ad revenue on most sites.

Post-paragraph #1Sticky sidebarMobile footer
06

Stack Affiliate Income

For every article, find a relevant affiliate product. Finance: credit card links (up to $200/approval). Health: supplement affiliate programs (30–40% commission). Tech: software (recurring 20–30% commission). One good article can earn $500+/month in affiliate alone.

Amazon AssociatesShareASaleImpactCJ
07

Build Email List & Sell Directly

Add a lead magnet (free ebook, checklist, calculator). Email subscribers are worth $1–3/month each. A list of 5,000 people = $5,000–$15,000/month from sponsored emails, affiliate promos, and your own digital products — with zero ad network fees.

ConvertKitMailerLiteLead Magnet
Pro Tips

Maximize
Every Visit

⚡

Page Speed Matters

A 1-second delay = 7% drop in conversions. Faster pages rank higher, retain more visitors, and earn more ad impressions. Use a CDN and compress images.

📱

Mobile = 65% of Traffic

Mobile sticky bottom ads earn $6–$20 CPM vs $2–$4 for desktop banners. Always test your site on mobile first and optimize for it.

🔄

Update Old Articles

"Best [X] in 2026" articles get refreshed every year, bringing back old traffic. Google rewards fresh content with higher rankings and more impressions.

🌍

Target Q4 (Oct–Dec)

Advertisers spend 40–60% of their annual budget in Q4. RPMs can double or triple. Plan your best content for the fall push.
